How it works

Pre-event AI scan

SLAM mapping creates 3D venue models. AI identifies vulnerabilities and optimizes patrol routes for maximum coverage with minimal overlap.

Autonomous fleet patrols

Multiple drones per zone with seamless battery rotation. When one needs charging, another takes its place—no coverage gaps. Real-time person detection and intrusion alerts.

Safety Receipt

Comprehensive post-event report delivered within 24 hours, documenting all activities, incidents, and security recommendations.

Advanced Technology

AI Fleet Management

Central AI assigns routes, monitors battery levels, and handles seamless handoffs between drones. Predictive maintenance alerts ensure optimal performance.

Mobile Command Center

5G/Starlink connectivity with edge computing for real-time video streaming. Fast battery swap systems and on-site maintenance capabilities.

Smart Detection

Thermal cameras for night events, dynamic geofencing, and automatic collision avoidance. BVLOS operations with redundant communication links.
