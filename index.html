How It Works

1. Pre-Event Perimeter Plan

We survey your venue and create custom drone routes, launch points, and a communications plan tailored to your security team's workflow.

2. Live Overwatch + Alerts

During your event, our operators monitor drone feeds in real time. When we spot something, we verify and immediately hand off to your on-site security lead with exact location.

3. Next-Day Safety Receipt

Within 24 hours, you receive a complete timeline, coverage maps, incident log, and recommendations—proof of protection for stakeholders and insurers.
