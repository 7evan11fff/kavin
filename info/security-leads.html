Handoff Protocol

Detection

AI flags anomaly or operator identifies suspicious activity. Drone maintains visual contact. Incident classified: Vehicle approach, Person on foot, Group movement, Perimeter breach, Other.

Alert

Immediate radio call to your security lead with: location (zone/landmark), nature of activity, number of individuals/vehicles, current behavior, and recommendation. Target: under 30 seconds from detection to alert.

Track & Guide

Drone maintains overwatch while your ground team responds. We provide real-time updates: direction of movement, additional individuals, escape routes being taken. Continuous comms until your team has eyes-on.

Handoff Complete

Your team confirms visual contact. Drone resumes patrol pattern or remains on station per your request. Incident logged with timestamp, classification, response time, and outcome.

Operational Constraints

What We Don't Do

  • No over-crowd flights — Drones stay at perimeter
  • No physical intervention — We observe and report
  • No pursuit beyond perimeter — We hand off to ground
  • No law enforcement action — We're eyes, not enforcement

Weather Limitations

  • Wind: Operations pause above 25 mph sustained
  • Rain: Light rain OK, heavy rain grounds drones
  • Visibility: Minimum 3 miles visibility required
  • Lightning: Operations suspend, resume when clear

Airspace Compliance

  • FAA Part 107 — All operations fully compliant
  • LAANC clearance — Obtained pre-event as needed
  • TFRs — We monitor and comply with temporary restrictions
  • Altitude: Max 400 feet AGL, typically 150-250 feet
