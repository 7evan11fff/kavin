Privacy by Default

What We Record

Our drones monitor the perimeter—parking lots, access roads, surrounding areas—not the event itself. We're looking for potential threats approaching the venue, not documenting who's attending or what they're doing inside.

Perimeter-focused only
No interior coverage
No guest documentation

Who Can Access Footage

Access is strictly limited: our on-site operators during the event, and your designated security lead. Footage is not shared with event staff, other vendors, or third parties unless required by law or explicitly authorized by you.

Security lead access only
Encrypted storage
NDA available

Retention & Deletion

Standard retention is 30 days, after which footage is permanently deleted. Need it deleted sooner? We accommodate expedited deletion requests. Need it retained longer for legal/insurance purposes? We can arrange secure extended storage.

30-day standard retention
Expedited deletion available
Extended storage on request

Additional Privacy Options

Face Blur Processing

For high-profile events where footage may be reviewed by multiple parties, we offer automated face-blur processing on all recorded footage before delivery. Faces become unidentifiable while security-relevant details remain visible.

  • Automated AI-powered blur
  • Applied before footage leaves our systems
  • Preserves security-relevant detail
  • Available on all package tiers

Custom NDAs & Agreements

We sign your NDA. If you have specific confidentiality requirements from your legal or security team, we work with them directly to ensure our operations meet your standards before the first drone takes off.

  • Sign your NDA
  • Custom confidentiality terms
  • Work directly with your legal team
  • Pre-event compliance verification

Vetted Personnel

Our pilots and operators undergo background checks. For events with elevated security requirements, we can provide personnel files to your security team in advance and accommodate additional vetting requirements.

  • Personnel files available on request
  • Additional vetting accommodated
