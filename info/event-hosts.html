How Event Day Runs

Before Guests Arrive

Our mobile command vehicle arrives 2-3 hours before your event opens. We set up in a designated area (roughly 2 parking spaces), conduct pre-flight checks, and coordinate with your security team on the day's communication protocol.

  • 30-minute setup time
  • Pre-event security briefing
  • Radio/phone coordination established
  • Test flights complete before doors open

During the Event

Drones patrol continuously with seamless battery rotations—no gaps in coverage. Our operators monitor feeds and AI alerts from the command vehicle. You won't hear us; guests typically don't notice the drones at perimeter altitude.

  • Continuous perimeter patrols
  • Zero coverage gaps
  • Low-profile operations
  • Instant response to incidents

After the Event

Once the last guest departs and your security gives the all-clear, we pack up and leave. Within 24 hours, your Safety Receipt arrives via email with full documentation of the operation.

  • Clean departure
  • Next-day report delivery
  • Footage retention per agreement
  • Debrief call available on request
